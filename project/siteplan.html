Brew Haven Café

A cozy coffee shop website for coffee lovers.

Site Name

Brew Haven Café

I chose this name because it sounds warm, relaxing, and welcoming, which matches the atmosphere of a coffee shop.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about the coffee shop, including menu items, opening hours, featured drinks, customer reviews, and contact information.

Scenarios

  • What drinks are available on the café menu?
  • What time does the coffee shop open and close?

Color Schema

The chosen colors reflect the warm and cozy atmosphere of coffee.

#5c3d2e
#d4a373
  • #5c3d2e will be used for headings, navigation, and footer backgrounds.
  • #d4a373 will be used for accents, buttons, and highlights.

Typography

The website will use the Google Font Poppins.

  • Poppins will be used for headings and body text.
